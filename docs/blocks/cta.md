# CTA Block

## Purpose
The final call to action at the bottom of the funnel. Should immediately communicate **Action and Urgency**.

## Variants
- `centered`: A full-width, highly prominent section that demands attention.

## Editable Fields
- `title` (text)
- `subtitle` (textarea)
- `primaryButtonText` (text)

## Responsive Notes
- Typography scales fluidly.
- Padding decreases on smaller screens to keep content visible without scrolling.

## Accessibility
- Uses `slate-900` or a deep brand color for high contrast against white text.
- Large click targets.
